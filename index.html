About Us ✨

Hatoeta is the Arabic word for "Story." With Hatoeta, we aim to share our stories. Central to this story are two elements — looking back and looking forward:

Honoring Tradition

We celebrate the Palestinian-Jordanian culinary heritage of our grandparents. Many dishes are shared in this tradition, making Hatoeta a “sharing” restaurant. It's best to order 2-3 dishes per person, but of course, if you prefer a plate for yourself, that's perfectly fine.

Focus on Sustainability

We actively pursue dishes with a low environmental impact. You'll find many vegetarian and vegan options here, reflecting our commitment to sustainability and caring for our planet.
